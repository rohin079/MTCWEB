Greetings from Microsoft Technical Community, Hope you all are doing good. As we begin with the new semester, MTC is coming up with yet another event "Success Stories". This event will be conducted on the 18th of January 2023 at 8:30pm virtually.
This is going to be a panel discussion which consists of Rashi Trikha Ma'am, Vishal Gupta Sir, Hardik Garg Sir, Somasekhar M. Sir and Kartikeya Khanna Sir . Who are currently in their Final Year and got placed in the top companies. They will be sharing their experiences of their interview preparations and also solving doubts asked by freshers. On top of it, they will also be guiding participants about importance of an effective CV/ Resume and how to build one.
 So if you are confused about your interviews and placements and are often worried about the preparations. This session is for you. Mark the date and tune us on 18th January 2023.
