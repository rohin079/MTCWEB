TECHFUSE: CYBER SECURITY


Greetings Tech Enthusiasts!!

Are you in your first year and still figuring out which specialization to choose? Worry not you are in luck!! Microsoft Technical Community (MTC) is hosting a series of workshops called TechFuse 1.0 for you. These workshops are designed to give you a deeper understanding of the different specializations, including AI/ML, Cyber Security, and Forensics.

The first workshop was focused onCloud Computing which was presented by Mr. Sanidhya Soni and was a great success.

Now Microsoft Technical Community is conducting its second workshop based on Cyber Security, and attendees will have the chance to learn from a Cyber Security and Forensics expert with significant knowledge in the field. The speaker Mr. Akshit Sharma is a former PR head of the Microsoft Technical Community and is currently a final year B. Tech Computer Science student at UPES Dehradun.

Don't miss the chance to acknowledge yourself about the world of Cyber Security and dive into its depths. It will be an online event keeping in consideration your comfort. We hope to see you there!!


Further Details:
Date : 11th March 2023
Time : 4:00 PM- 6:00 PM
Venue: Online
We hope you will take advantage of this opportunity to enhance your cybersecurity knowledge and skills. Thank you again for your interest, and we look forward to seeing you at the workshop.
